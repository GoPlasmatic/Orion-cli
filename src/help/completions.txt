Supported Shells: bash, zsh, fish, powershell, elvish

Installation:
  Bash:        orion-cli completions bash >> ~/.bashrc
  Zsh:         orion-cli completions zsh > "${fpath[1]}/_orion-cli"
  Fish:        orion-cli completions fish > ~/.config/fish/completions/orion-cli.fish
  PowerShell:  orion-cli completions powershell >> $PROFILE
